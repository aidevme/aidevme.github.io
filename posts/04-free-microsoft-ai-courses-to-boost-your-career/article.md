    Perfect for newbies! This 12-week course covers key AI concepts like NLP and deep learning, with hands-on coding labs for real-life skills. Free courses like these offer excellent access to AI knowledge thanks to Microsoft.  
    [Course Link](https://lnkd.in/gbyGV3Ni)
4.  **Transform Your Business with Microsoft AI**  
    A strategic four-part program aimed at leaders. It focuses on planning and scaling AI initiatives while emphasizing responsible AI principles.  
    [Course Link](https://lnkd.in/gbzY-6GX)
5.  **AI for Managers**  
    This course is designed for managers looking to apply generative AI in daily tasks like feedback and decision-making, grounded in best practices.  
    [Course Link](https://lnkd.in/gbgZmcGP)
6.  **Using Azure OpenAI Service**  
    A technical introduction to Microsoft’s Azure OpenAI tools, perfect for those wanting to dive deep into prompt design and model output enhancement. Discovering free Microsoft AI courses can open many doors.  
    [Course Link](https://lnkd.in/gY59A52m)
7.  **Build Apps with Azure & Power Virtual Agents**  
    For the hands-on learners out there, this course teaches you to build AI-powered apps and chatbots using Microsoft’s tools.

8.  **Create Custom Machine Learning Models**  
    Learn to build, train, and deploy machine-learning models using Microsoft’s comprehensive toolset. Free courses enhance learning with practical applications.  
    [Course Link](https://lnkd.in/gdzut-iy)
9.  **Adopting Copilot for Microsoft 365**  
    This practical course shows how to use Copilot across various Microsoft applications to simplify your workflow.  
    [Course Link](https://lnkd.in/gAjvhbiv)
